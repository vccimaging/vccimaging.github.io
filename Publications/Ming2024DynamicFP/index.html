Principle of dynamic FP via spacetime framework. (a) Overall pipeline. (b) Experimental setup.

Abstract

We introduce a dynamic Fourier ptychography (FP) technique via a spacetime framework, jointly recovering object states and deformation fields for detailed observation of rapid, complex behaviors in living cells.
